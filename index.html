Welcome to my homepage. I am an assistant researcher at the Department of Network Intelligence in Pengcheng Lab .

Before joining Pengcheng Lab, I worked as a wireless researcher/research scientist in Wireless Network Research Department, led by Dr. Chen SUN, Research and Development Center (China Laboratory), SONY. In SONY, I also served as a 3GPP SA2 delegate in SONY.

I received my bachelor's degree from Harbin Institute of Technology in 2013 and my master's degree from Xi'an Jiaotong University in 2016, respectively. Thanks to my supervisors Prof. Laurent CLAVIER and Dr. Malcolm EGAN, I defended my Ph.D thesis "Impulsive and Dependent Interference in IoT Networks" on 8th December, 2020. During my Ph.D, I was also supervised by Prof. Jean-Marie GORCE and Prof. Gareth W. Peters. In 2019, I got a chance to visit Aalborg University, hosted by Prof. Troels PEDERSEN and Prof. Petar POPOVSKI.

My Ph.D. work includes impulsive and dependent interference (alpha-stable distribution and copula theory) and stochastic geometry.

My research in SONY focuses on Federated Learning (FL), D2D sidelink, Vehicle Mound Relay (VMR), etc.

I am currently focusing on the combination of Large Language Models (LLM) and Networking!
